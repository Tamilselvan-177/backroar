## üóÑÔ∏è DATABASE STRUCTURE

### **Main Tables:**

1. **users** - Customer & admin accounts
2. **categories** - 4 main categories (Phones, Backcases, Gadgets, Tempered Glass)
3. **subcategories** - 13 backcase types, 9 gadget types, 10 tempered glass types
4. **brands** - iPhone, Samsung, Vivo, Oppo, Redmi, OnePlus, Moto, Realme, Lava, Infinix
5. **models** - Phone models for each brand
6. **products** - All products with category, subcategory, brand, model relationships
7. **product_images** - Product images (stored in batch folders)
8. **addresses** - User delivery addresses
9. **cart_items** - Shopping cart
10. **orders** - Order master table
11. **order_items** - Order line items
12. **payment_logs** - Payment tracking

### **Key Relationships:**
- Products ‚Üí Categories (1:many)
- Products ‚Üí Subcategories (1:many)
- Products ‚Üí Brands (1:many)
- Products ‚Üí Models (1:many)
- Orders ‚Üí Users (1:many)
- Orders ‚Üí Order Items (1:many)
- Cart Items ‚Üí Products (1:many)
